# Central Product Theme (CPT)

## Product Vision
To be the definitive cornhole tournament companion for church communities, merging mobile convenience with the drama of competitive play.

## Value Proposition
Bags & Brats simplifies tournament logistics while amplifying engagement through "The Big Reveal" - a managed suspense mechanic for tournament climaxes.

## Target Users
- **Church Families**: Casual and competitive players accessing via mobile.
- **Ministry Leaders**: Tournament directors needing "big screen" presentation tools.

## Core Jobs-to-be-Done
- **Flexible Identity**: Register with name, email, and phone, or use Google Auth for speed.
- **Admin Orchestration**: Define multi-week tournament dates and notify all registrants via system messaging.
- **Presence Verification**: Self-service check-in window (up to 30 mins before) to ensure accurate daily pairings.
- **Inclusion Measures**: Admin "Proxy" registration for players without accounts or mobile phones.
- **Dynamic Team Formation**: Randomize teams daily from *checked-in* players, avoiding repeat pairings.
- **The Big Reveal**: Admin-controlled visibility to hide final results until the big announcement.
