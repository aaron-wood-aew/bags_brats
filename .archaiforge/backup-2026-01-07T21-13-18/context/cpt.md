# Central Product Theme (CPT)

## Product Vision
To be the definitive real-time cornhole tournament companion for church communities, merging mobile convenience with the drama of live, synchronized competitive play.

## Value Proposition
Bags & Brats simplifies tournament logistics while amplifying engagement through "Gameday Pulse" - a suite of real-time orchestration tools and "The Big Reveal" managed suspense mechanics.

## Target Users
- **Church Families**: Casual and competitive players accessing via mobile for registration, check-in, and personal stats.
- **Ministry Leaders**: Tournament directors utilizing "Big Screen" presentation tools (Spectator View) to drive community engagement.

## Core Jobs-to-be-Done
- **Flexible Identity (Phase 9)**: Swift registration via name/email or Multi-Method OAuth (Google & Apple) for cross-platform ease.
- **Admin Orchestration**: Define multi-week tournament dates and notify all registrants via system messaging.
- **Presence Verification**: Self-service check-in window (up to 30 mins before) to ensure accurate daily pairings.
- **Gameday Pulse**: Real-time match synchronization using absolute UTC to eliminate drift, featuring visual "Big Screen" cues for time-sensitive events.
- **Dynamic Team Formation**: Randomize teams daily from *checked-in* players, avoiding repeat pairings.
- **The Big Reveal**: Admin-controlled visibility to hide final results until the big announcement, maintaining suspense in personal dashboards.
